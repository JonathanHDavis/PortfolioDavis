Who Ruled Them All?

Comparing the Lord of the Rings Films

"The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring"

This movie took the world by storm. No one had seen a movie quite like this before.

It combined drama, action, political intrigue, and even magic and imaginary creatures. It brought together all types of people who could each find something to enjoy.

The old and young, the nerdy and cool, the main stream and unique, could each see this movie and pinpoint a specific part they enjoyed above the rest.

"The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers"

The second chapter cranked everything up about 1,000 notches.

As much as the scale was trememndous in the first one, this movie added so much more. There is a battle sequence that lasts about 45 minutes and it is absolutely mind blowing.

What people admire most about these movies is in these huge battles, there were very few visual effects, it was mostly done through hard work with practical camera tricks.

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King"

This movie man, this freaking movie. AMAZING!

The trilogy ends so perfectly with this final chapter. Everything the first two did this one combined and added in closure and more incredible drama.

It made all of the money and has the record for most Academy Awards for any one film, including Best Picture and Best Director.

No movie like it has ever had this much commercial and critical success, and it is a testament to what a nearly flawless project this entire series came to be.
